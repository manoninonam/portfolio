At TechGrounds I've learned the foundations of HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. During these six months I coded day in day out, week after week. We were teached how to adequately problem solve and how to learn self-directed and with our peers. Aside from the languages I just stated, I also worked with tools as Redux, Firebase and Bootstrap.
